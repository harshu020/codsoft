Vikram Batra

1974-1999

Yeh Dil Maange More.

Biography

  • Vikram Batra was born on 9 September 1974, in a Punjabi Khatri family in a small town in Palampur, Himachal Pradesh.
  • He was the third child of Girdhari Lal Batra.
  • Vikram Batra was an officer in the Indian Army.
  • He was given the Param Vir Chakra, India's highest military medal.
  • After he died for his actions during the Kargil War.
  • It was given to him on July 7, 1999.
  • He was commissioned into the Indian Army on 6 December 1997 in the 13 Jammu and Kashmir Rifles (JAK RIF).
